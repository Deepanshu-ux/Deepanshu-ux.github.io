Pre-Season Training Kicks Off at JCC Ground

July 15, 2025 — Our players have started intense pre-season training at the JCC Ground A in preparation for the upcoming matches. The camp focuses on strength conditioning and tactical drills.

August 20, 2025 — The final squad for the September fixtures has been announced. Star players like Raphinha and Neymar Jr. will be leading the team against JIIMS SEC-5 and JIIMS Noida.

JCC Wins Friendly Match Against Local Rivals

August 5, 2025 — In a thrilling friendly game, JCC beat the rival team from North Delhi with a score of 3-1. Rodrygo scored twice and was declared the player of the match.
